Website Overview

BH Motors is a classified website showcasing three premium car models: Sedan, SUV, and Coupe. Each car has its own dedicated page with specifications and details. The site includes a registration form, mock-up justifications, and a critical reflection on the development process. This project follows strict guidelines including validation, user personalization, and structured content across six pages.
